What's It All About?

FestiFall is a one-day premier music experience set among lush wooded landscapes in Tromsø, northern Norway, featuring renowned headliners and emerging artists. Witness unforgettable performances and discover the engaging attractions nestled around every turn that make FestiFall a can’t-miss fall tradition for music fans from around the world.

FAQ

What are the age restrictions?

Under 18’s must be accompanied by an adult aged 21 or over. One adult can accompany up to 3 under 18s.

What is the line up?

The full schedule will be announced closer to the event and updates will be posted on the line-up page.
