What's It All About?

FestiFall is a one-day premier music experience set among lush wooded landscapes in Tromsø, northern Norway, featuring renowned headliners and emerging artists. Witness unforgettable performances and discover the engaging attractions nestled around every turn that make FestiFall a can’t-miss fall tradition for music fans from around the world. Tromsø is based on an island surrounded by majestic mountains which are covered in snow most of the year. About 70,000 people live here and 10,000 of those are students.
